In the past week I’ve discovered two very interesting and awesome things. One is browser based gaming, sure I’ve been told about it, but wow it’s awesome once youa ctually try. This has got to be the best MMORPG I’ve ever played. It’s got all the properties of a MMORPG, except perhaps for an active environment, but in the mean time it’s lacking all of the bad properties. It doesn’t eat up all of your time and it certainly doesn’t glue you to the computer screen for hours on end. On top of that Imperia Online has very nice hand drawn images that are simply marvelous to look at.

Another great discovery was Death & Maiden a very funny webcomic that speaks of Death being quite the interesting and complex character that is getting cheated by a mere girly.
